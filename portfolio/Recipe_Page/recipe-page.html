Mom's First Spaghetti.

What's so special about this spaghetti?

The first time my mom made spaghetti, it was because her mom was out and needed somebody else to make dinner that night. My mom was the oldest kid and so the responsibility fell to her. Grandma thought, "What's so hard about spaghetti?" and my mom showed her.

My mom did not know why her mom provided two pots and simply cooked it all in one pot.

Long story short, she was never asked to make dinner again.

What you need

Ingredients

  • 1 jar of Red sauce
  • 1 box of uncooked Spaghetti

Equipment

  • Stove
  • One pot
  • Plates
  • Spoon

Steps

  1. Put pot on stove
  2. Turn on burner
  3. Pour sauce in pot
  4. Add uncooked spaghetti to pot
  5. Optional: Stir
  6. Cook until it's dinnertime
  7. Serve
    (we recommend providing steak knives or a trash bag)

Warnings & Recommendations

If you're looking for good ways to make pasta, don't do this recipe. You deserve better.


Can you eat raw pasta?

We do not recommend eating uncooked pasta. It probably won't kill you, but it's still not the best choice you can make.
